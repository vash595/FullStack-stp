FAQs








1. What is the best time to visit Rajasthan?
The best time to visit is between October and March, when the weather is pleasant and perfect for sightseeing.




2. Do I need a visa to travel to India?
Yes, most foreign nationals require a valid visa to enter India. You can apply for an e-Visa online for tourism purposes.




3. Is Rajasthan safe for tourists?
Yes, Rajasthan is generally safe for tourists. However, like anywhere, it's smart to stay aware of your surroundings and follow local guidelines.




4. What are the must-visit cities in Rajasthan?
Top cities include Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer, Bikaner, and Ajmer, each offering a unique blend of culture, history, and architecture.




5. What kind of clothing should I pack?
Pack light cotton clothes for the summer, and warm layers for winter (especially December-January). Modest clothing is appreciated at religious sites.




6. Are guided tours available?
Yes, we offer multilingual guided tours, private drivers, and local experts to enhance your experience.




7. Can I book transportation and accommodation through your website?
Absolutely! You can book hotels, local transport, airport pickups, and even full travel packages directly through our platform.




8. Do you offer custom travel itineraries?
Yes! Our team can help build a custom itinerary based on your interests, time, and budget.




9. What modes of transport are available within Rajasthan?
You can travel via trains, buses, taxis, and private cabs. Major cities are also connected by domestic flights.




10. What are some popular local dishes to try?
Don’t miss Dal Baati Churma, Laal Maas, Gatte ki Sabzi, and Ker Sangri—authentic Rajasthani food is a treat for the senses!
