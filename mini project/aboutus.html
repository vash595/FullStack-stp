About Us





At Sitata, we believe travel should be more than just ticking off places — it should be about stories, vibes, and unforgettable moments. We’re your local travel crew, here to help you explore the heart of Rajasthan like a true insider. From epic forts and colorful bazaars to desert safaris and soulful temples, we curate the best experiences, stays, and guides to make your trip smooth and stress-free. Whether you're chasing sunsets in Jaisalmer or street food in Jaipur, Sitata’s got your back — because Rajasthan isn’t just a place, it’s a feeling.








Our Story

Sitata started with one simple thought — why just visit a place when you can truly feel it? Born from countless road trips, chai breaks, and late-night travel chats, Sitata was created by a bunch of explorers who fell in love with the raw, unfiltered beauty of Rajasthan. We saw travelers missing out on the real magic — the hidden gems, the local legends, the food stalls only locals know — and we knew we had to change that. So, we built Sitata to be more than a travel site. It’s your Rajasthan insider, storyteller, and guide. Every tour, every photo, and every tip on Sitata comes from real experiences and a genuine love for this land. This is our story, but we’re here to help you write yours — one unforgettable trip at a time.
